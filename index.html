About Me

My name is Adam Duff, and I am an up and coming front-end web developer. I spent much of middle school years (some 20 years ago) designing my own websites, and somewhere along the way, I stopped doing it. So, here I am today, more intrigued by web development than ever!

It truly is amazing, looking back, at how much things have changed. Back in "the day," we only had to account for desktop sizes and cross-browser compatibility. These days, everyone is viewing your website on different devices, with many different screen sizes, and truly has morphed the way we go about website design and development.

My intent with this website is just a spot to share the projects I am working on, and I may add a blog/updates page at some point, if I am able to justify it, just to share my thoughts. For now, this is pretty barebones, and knowing me, will see many evolutions.

My end goal is to obtain a career in web development. I have been studying on and off over the last year, and I can safely say I have not only refreshed my memory on how all this stuff works, I am now more knowledgable on the subject than I have ever been before. I was lucky enough to obtain the Grow with Google Scholarship through Udacity, and I am working hard on completing that. From there, who knows what is in store! It is a journey and all journeys start somewhere. For now, it starts here.
